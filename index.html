What we do

Formwork services

Traditional and system formwork for residential, commercial and civil concrete work.

  • Concrete stairs

    Cast in-situ staircases — straight flights, dog-legs and helical — formed cleanly, set true, finished to a high standard.

  • Walls & retaining walls

    Plumb, tight, well-tied wall forms for basements, retaining structures and feature concrete finishes.

  • Slabs & foundations

    Suspended slabs, ground bearing slabs, pile caps and foundations — set out accurately and poured cleanly.

  • Columns

    Square, rectangular and circular columns — properly braced forms with crisp arrises and a clean fair-faced finish where needed.

  • Reinforced concrete structures

    Frames, cores and decks — coordinated with the steel fixers and concrete gangs to keep the programme moving.

  • System & traditional formwork

    Comfortable on both modular system kit and bespoke timber formwork — whichever suits the job and the finish required.

About us

A small Bath-based formwork firm.

NH Formwork Limited is a hands-on, family-run formwork outfit based in Bath. We work day in, day out on concrete stairs, walls, slabs and reinforced structures across the South West.

We keep the team tight and the standards high. Whether it’s a single staircase in a private home or a frame on a commercial site, we treat every form like it’s the one that matters most — because for the people standing on the concrete afterwards, it is.
