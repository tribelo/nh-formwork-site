About NH Formwork

Bath-based. Hands-on.

NH Formwork Limited is a small, hands-on team of structural concrete specialists based in Bath. Since 2018 we’ve been delivering rebar, concrete and formwork to builders, contractors and homeowners across the South West.

The people on site are the people you speak to on the phone. Our in-house crew sets out the works, fixes the steel, builds the formwork, pours the concrete and finishes it — start to finish, on one team. No subbing-out the parts that matter.

From a single-flight concrete stair to a full reinforced-concrete frame, the same hands stay on the job until it’s done.

  • Craftsmanship first. Concrete that lines up with the drawings — and the next trade.
  • Programme honest. If we say it’s in by Friday, it’s in by Friday.
  • Fully insured. Public and employer’s liability cover, current at every job.
